# Top YouTube Day Traders - Strategy Research

## 1. Ross Cameron (Warrior Trading)
**Channel:** @DaytradeWarrior  
**Track Record:** Turned $583 into $10M+ (not typical results)

**Strategy: Momentum Scalping**
- **Timeframe:** 1-minute and 5-minute charts, occasionally 10-second
- **Focus:** Small-cap stocks with 20-30%+ moves, high volume
- **Bias:** Long-biased (front side of moves only)
- **Setup:** Top 10 gappers/gainers daily

**Entry Rules:**
1. Stock must be in strong directional movement
2. High relative volume (not choppy)
3. Clean price action (no dojis/reversals)
4. Momentum confirmed on 1-min chart

**Exit Rules:**
1. Cut losses QUICK (5-10 seconds of stalling)
2. Take profit at targets or momentum stall
3. Avoid backside (declining) of moves
4. 1-2 dojis = immediate exit

**Risk Management:**
- Tight stops (cut immediately on hesitation)
- Small position sizes (scalp = quick in/out)
- Only trade A+ setups

**Assets:** Small-cap stocks with news catalysts, low float

## 3. Adam Khoo
**Channel:** Adam Khoo  
**Style:** Swing + Day Trading Hybrid

**Strategy: Multi-Timeframe Analysis**
- **Timeframes:** Daily (trend), 1-hour (setup), 15-min (entry)
- **Approach:** Technical + Fundamental + Psychology
- **Indicators:** EMA, Support/Resistance, Chart Patterns

**Entry Rules:**
1. Daily trend established
2. Pullback to support/EMA
3. Confirmation candle on lower timeframe
4. Risk/reward 1:3 or better

**Exit Rules:**
1. Stop loss below support
2. Take profit at next resistance
3. Trailing stop once in profit

**Risk Management:**
- 1-2% risk per trade
- Position sizing based on volatility
- Portfolio heat management

**Assets:** Large caps, forex, crypto
